At Skiza Spin & Win, we are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal personal information necessary to operate the promotion:

  • Phone Number: Your Kenyan mobile phone number for M-Pesa payments
  • Session Data: Technical information about your visit (IP address, browser type)
  • Prize Information: The amount you won and withdrawal status
  • Subscription Status: Verification of your Skiza subscription

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Prize Distribution: To send your winnings via M-Pesa
  • Verification: To verify your Skiza subscription status
  • Communication: To send SMS notifications about your withdrawal status
  • Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Service Improvement: To improve our promotional services

We DO NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Safaricom/M-Pesa: To process prize payments and verify subscriptions
  • Legal Compliance: When required by Kenyan law or legal proceedings
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who assist in operating our service (under strict confidentiality agreements)

We will NEVER sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary:

  • Active Requests: Until your withdrawal is completed
  • Completed Withdrawals: For 90 days after payment
  • Legal Requirements: As required by Kenyan law for tax or audit purposes
  • Session Data: Automatically deleted after 30 days

5. Your Rights

Under Kenyan data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Right to request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Right to request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Objection: Right to object to processing of your data
  • Portability: Right to receive your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@skizapromo.co.ke

6. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Session Management: To track your spin and withdrawal status
  • Security: To protect against fraud and unauthorized access
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our service

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect service functionality.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Staff training on data protection
  • CSRF protection on all forms

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it immediately.

9. Compliance with Kenyan Law

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019
  • Communications Authority of Kenya regulations
  • Consumer Protection Act

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
